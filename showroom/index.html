About Our Space

Sydney's Largest Wallpaper Display

Our Kellyville Ridge showroom is purpose-built for wallpaper. Step inside and you'll find over 2,000 wallpaper samples arranged by style — from contemporary geometrics and botanical prints to classic textures, luxury metallics, and children's designs.

Unlike online shopping, visiting our showroom lets you see, touch, and compare wallpapers in natural light. Bruce is always on hand to offer expert advice on pattern selection, colour matching, and suitability for your space. We'll help you choose the perfect wallpaper and provide a quote on the spot.

No appointment needed — just walk in. We're open 7 days a week, 9 am to 5 pm. Free parking available.
