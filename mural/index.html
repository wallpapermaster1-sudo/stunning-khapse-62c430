Custom Printed

Bespoke Wallpaper Murals

Transform any wall into a one-of-a-kind statement piece. We print custom wallpaper murals to your exact dimensions using any image, artwork, or design you provide.

The Service

Your Vision, Your Wall

A custom wallpaper mural is the ultimate way to personalise your space. Unlike standard wallpaper patterns, a mural is printed specifically for your wall — meaning it fits perfectly, with no repeats and no compromise.

Send us any high-resolution image — a favourite photograph, digital artwork, an abstract design, or even a hand-drawn sketch — and we'll print it as a full-wall mural to your exact measurements. The result is a stunning, seamless feature wall that's uniquely yours.

Our murals are printed on premium non-woven material, ensuring vivid colour reproduction, durability, and easy installation. And like all our work, professional installation by our experienced team is included.

How It Works

From Idea to Installed

01

Share Your Vision

Send us your image, artwork, or design idea. Not sure what you want? Visit our showroom and we'll help you explore options and find inspiration.

02

Measure & Quote

Provide your wall dimensions (or we can measure for you). We'll create a digital preview showing exactly how the mural will look on your wall, and provide a fixed-price quote.

03

Print & Produce

Once approved, your mural is printed on premium non-woven material using high-definition digital printing. Production typically takes 5–7 business days.

04

Install & Enjoy

Our professional installers handle everything — wall prep, precise alignment, and clean-up. The result is a flawless, seamless mural that transforms your space.
