Helpful Guides

Tips & Preparation

How to Measure

Getting accurate measurements is key to a successful wallpaper project.

  1. Measure the total width of all walls being papered (in metres)
  2. Measure the height from floor to ceiling
  3. Note the position and size of any doors or windows
  4. Add 10% for waste and pattern matching

Wall Preparation

Proper wall prep is essential for a lasting, professional result.

  1. Remove any existing wallpaper completely
  2. Fill holes and cracks, then sand smooth
  3. Clean the wall to remove dust and grease
  4. Apply a coat of wallpaper primer or sizing

Choosing the Right Wallpaper

Different rooms have different needs. Here's a quick guide.

  1. Living areas: Any type — choose what you love
  2. Bathrooms: Vinyl or moisture-resistant papers
  3. Kitchens: Scrubbable or wipeable finishes
  4. Kids' rooms: Durable, washable options
