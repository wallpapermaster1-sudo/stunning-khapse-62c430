About Wallpaper Masters

Sydney's Wallpaper Experts, Est. 2006

Wallpaper Masters Pty Ltd is a Sydney-based wallpaper supply and professional installation company founded by Bruce Choi in 2006. For nearly two decades, we have transformed homes and commercial spaces across greater Sydney with premium quality wallpaper and expert craftsmanship.

From our Kellyville Ridge showroom, we display over 2,000 wallpaper samples — from modern geometric designs and classic textures to bold statement murals. Whether you need a single feature wall or a full home fit-out, our experienced team handles everything from pattern selection through to flawless installation.
